Decentralized Matchmaking on GenLayer

You've just stumbled upon the world's first blockchain-powered matchmaking service. No swipe algorithms. No creepy ads. Just cold, hard, decentralized consensus deciding who's perfect for you.

HeartConsensus runs on GenLayer — a next-gen blockchain where AI validators don't just process transactions, they deliberate. Like a jury. For your love life.

1
Answer 10 questions. Tell us about yourself — your hobbies, quirks, and maybe your worst habits. The more honest, the better the result.
2
Validators deliberate. Multiple AI nodes on GenLayer analyze your profile and reach a decentralized consensus on your ideal match. They might disagree. That's the point.
3
Meet your match. You receive a unique character — a name, a personality description, and a portrait. Generated on-chain. Certified by consensus. Slightly ridiculous.

No real relationships were harmed in the making of this product. Results may vary. Validators are not responsible for heartbreak.
